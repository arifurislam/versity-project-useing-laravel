OBAC, short for Outward Bound Adventure Club, the club, as the name suggests, stands for the exploration of the multifaceted boundaries of a man's physical, mental, and moral development. Among college students so physical, mental, moral
Along with the development, various exploratory thoughts or endeavors were developed, for this purpose the Club was launched in the mid-sixties under the inspiration of Father Richard William Timm, CSC (Magsaysay Awardee, 1987 AD). Mainly to know the unknown, to develop the inquisitive heart and mind of the students through knowing the unknown and to do something about social responsibility is the aim and objective of this club.
